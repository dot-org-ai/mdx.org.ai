# StoryBrand Framework: hacker.news.org.ai

## 1. Character (The Hero)

**Who is our customer?**

The Tech Professional who reads Hacker News but struggles to keep up with the volume of content.

**What do they want?**
- Summaries of long articles and threads
- Personalized feed based on interests
- Insights and trends from discussions
- Less time scrolling, more time learning

## 2. Problem

### External Problem (Tangible)
"There's too much on Hacker News. I can't read everything I want to."

### Internal Problem (Emotional)
"I feel like I'm missing important discussions. FOMO is real."

### Philosophical Problem (Why It's Wrong)
You shouldn't have to choose between staying informed and having time for actual work.

### The Villain
**Information Overload** - The flood of posts, comments, and threads that makes Hacker News overwhelming.

## 3. Guide (The Brand)

**Position:** We've built the AI layer on top of HN that filters, summarizes, and personalizes.

**Empathy:** We refresh Hacker News too many times a day. We built this to get the value without the addiction.

**Authority:**
- AI summaries of every post and thread
- Personalized feed learning your interests
- Trend detection across discussions
- Used by [X] HN readers daily

## 4. Plan (The Path)

### Step 1: Connect
Sign in with Hacker News (optional) or start with your interests.

### Step 2: Personalize
Tell us your areas of interest. AI learns from what you read.

### Step 3: Consume
Get summaries, curated content, and insights—all the value, less of the noise.

## 5. Call to Action

**Direct CTA:** Try Your Personalized Feed

**Transitional CTA:** See Today's Top Insights

## 6. Success (What's at Stake)

**Success looks like:**
- Staying informed in less time
- Not missing important discussions
- Deeper understanding from summaries
- Curated content matching your interests

**Transformation:**
From: HN reader drowning in content
To: HN reader efficiently informed

## 7. Failure (What We Help Them Avoid)

**Failure looks like:**
- Hours lost to HN scrolling
- Missing discussions you'd care about
- Reading long threads for buried insights
- FOMO driving unproductive habits

## 8. Brand Identity

### Voice
Informed, concise, insightful

### Tone
Sharp, helpful, efficient

### Key Messages
1. "All of HN. None of the FOMO."
2. "AI reads the threads so you don't have to."
3. "Your personalized tech news digest."
4. "Stay informed in 5 minutes, not 2 hours."

## 9. Features

### Summaries
- Article TL;DR before clicking through
- Thread highlights and key takeaways
- Controversy and consensus markers

### Personalization
- Interest-based feed
- "More like this" recommendations
- Hide topics you don't care about

### Insights
- Trending topics across discussions
- Sentiment analysis on launches
- Expert comment highlighting

## 10. Messaging by Audience

### For Engineers
"Read HN efficiently. AI summaries of posts and threads, personalized to your stack."

### For Founders
"Know what the HN community thinks about your space. Trends, sentiment, opportunities."

### For Everyone
"All the valuable discussions, none of the time sink. HN optimized for busy people."
