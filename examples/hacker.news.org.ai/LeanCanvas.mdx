# Lean Canvas: hacker.news.org.ai

## Problem

1. **Information Overload** - Too many posts to read, too little time
2. **Thread Noise** - Key insights buried in 300+ comment threads
3. **FOMO** - Missing important discussions in your areas

### Existing Alternatives
- Hacker News (original, no AI)
- HN aggregators (basic filtering)
- Newsletter digests (delayed, limited)
- Social following (incomplete)

## Customer Segments

### Early Adopters
- **Tech professionals** who refresh HN daily
- **Founders** tracking industry sentiment
- **Developers** staying current with trends

### Mainstream
- **Engineering managers** keeping up with tech
- **Tech recruiters** understanding the market
- **Investors** monitoring sentiment

## Unique Value Proposition

**All of HN. None of the FOMO.**

AI summarizes posts and threads, personalized to your interests. Stay informed in minutes, not hours.

### High-Level Concept
"AI-enhanced tech news consumption"

## Solution

1. **Smart Summaries** - AI TL;DR for posts and threads
2. **Personalized Feed** - Content matched to your interests
3. **Key Insights** - Highlight expert comments and consensus
4. **Trend Alerts** - Know when topics are trending
