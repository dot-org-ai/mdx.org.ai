## When to Use

- Landing page primary section
- Product launches
- Marketing campaigns
- App homepages

## Variations

### With Email Form

```html
<section data-section="hero">
  <h1>Stay in the loop</h1>
  <p>Get updates on our latest features.</p>
  <form data-email-form>
    <input type="email" placeholder="Enter your email" required />
    <button type="submit" data-button="primary">Subscribe</button>
  </form>
</section>
```
