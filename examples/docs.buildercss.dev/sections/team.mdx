## Semantic Pattern

A team section is a list of `<article>` elements with team member info:

```html
<section>
  <header>
    <h2>Meet Our Team</h2>
    <p>The people behind the product.</p>
  </header>

  <ul>
    <li>
      <article>
        <img src="/team/sarah.jpg" alt="Sarah Chen" />
        <h3>Sarah Chen</h3>
        <p>CEO & Co-founder</p>
        <nav aria-label="Social links">
          <a href="https://twitter.com/sarah">Twitter</a>
          <a href="https://linkedin.com/in/sarah">LinkedIn</a>
        </nav>
      </article>
    </li>

    <li>
      <article>
        <img src="/team/alex.jpg" alt="Alex Rivera" />
        <h3>Alex Rivera</h3>
        <p>CTO & Co-founder</p>
        <nav aria-label="Social links">
          <a href="https://twitter.com/alex">Twitter</a>
          <a href="https://github.com/alex">GitHub</a>
        </nav>
      </article>
    </li>
  </ul>
</section>
```

## CSS

```css
/* Team: section with ul > li > article containing figure + h3 */
section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) {
  padding: var(--spacing-16) var(--spacing-4);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) > header {
  text-align: center;
  margin-bottom: var(--spacing-12);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) > header h2 {
  font-size: var(--text-3xl);
  font-weight: 700;
  margin-bottom: var(--spacing-4);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) > header p {
  color: var(--muted-foreground);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) > ul {
  display: grid;
  grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(200px, 1fr));
  gap: var(--spacing-8);
  max-width: 900px;
  margin: 0 auto;
  list-style: none;
  padding: 0;
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article {
  text-align: center;
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article img,
section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article figure {
  width: 120px;
  height: 120px;
  border-radius: 50%;
  object-fit: cover;
  margin: 0 auto var(--spacing-4);
  background: var(--muted);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article h3 {
  font-weight: 600;
  margin-bottom: var(--spacing-1);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article > p {
  color: var(--muted-foreground);
  font-size: var(--text-sm);
  margin-bottom: var(--spacing-2);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article nav {
  display: flex;
  justify-content: center;
  gap: var(--spacing-2);
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article nav a {
  color: var(--muted-foreground);
  transition: color 0.2s;
}

section:has(> ul > li > article > figure + h3) article nav a:hover {
  color: var(--foreground);
}
```
