## When to Use

- Company blog index
- News archive pages
- Article listings
- Content hubs

## Structure

```html
<article data-page="blog">
  <header data-page-header>
    <h1>Blog</h1>
    <p>Insights, tutorials, and updates from our team</p>
  </header>

  <nav data-category-filter>
    <button data-active>All</button>
    <button>Engineering</button>
    <button>Design</button>
    <button>Product</button>
  </nav>

  <section data-blog-grid>
    <article data-blog-card>
      <img src="/post-image.jpg" data-card-image />
      <span data-category>Engineering</span>
      <h3>Building a Design System from Scratch</h3>
      <p data-meta>Dec 1, 2024 · 8 min read</p>
    </article>
  </section>

  <nav data-pagination>
    <button data-prev>←</button>
    <button data-page data-active>1</button>
    <button data-page>2</button>
    <button data-page>3</button>
    <button data-next>→</button>
  </nav>
</article>
```
