# Lean Canvas: blogg.ng

## Problem

1. **Blank Page Paralysis** - Staring at empty editors, never starting
2. **Consistency Failure** - Can't maintain regular publishing schedule
3. **Voice Inconsistency** - Generic AI content doesn't sound like you

### Existing Alternatives
- WordPress + ChatGPT (manual, disconnected)
- Jasper/Copy.ai (not trained on your voice)
- Ghost (no AI assistance)
- Medium (no ownership, no AI)

## Customer Segments

### Early Adopters
- **Solo content creators** with ideas but no time
- **Developer advocates** needing consistent content
- **Indie hackers** building in public

### Mainstream
- **Content marketing teams** scaling output
- **Thought leaders** establishing expertise
- **Business owners** doing their own marketing

## Unique Value Proposition

**Ideas In, Posts Out**

AI learns your voice and turns rough ideas into polished posts. Publish consistently without the grind.

### High-Level Concept
"AI blogging platform"

## Solution

1. **Voice Training** - AI learns from your past writing
2. **Idea Expansion** - Notes, outlines, voice memos become drafts
3. **SEO Optimization** - Keywords and structure built in
4. **Auto-Publishing** - Schedule and forget
