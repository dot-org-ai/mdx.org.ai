# Lean Canvas: app.net.ai

## Problem

1. **Development Barrier** - Building apps requires months of work and $50K+
2. **Validation Gap** - Can't test ideas without building them first
3. **Technical Complexity** - Even simple apps require many technologies

### Existing Alternatives
- Custom development (expensive, slow)
- No-code tools (limited, clunky UX)
- Mockup tools (not functional)
- Templates (still require coding)

## Customer Segments

### Early Adopters
- **Non-technical founders** with app ideas
- **Creators** wanting their own platforms
- **Developers** wanting faster prototyping

### Mainstream
- **Startup teams** validating concepts
- **Enterprise innovation** groups
- **Agencies** delivering rapid prototypes

## Unique Value Proposition

**Vibe Code Your App in Minutes**

Describe what you want. AI generates a complete, deployed application. Iterate by describing changes.

### High-Level Concept
"AI app generation via vibe coding"

## Solution

1. **Natural Language Input** - Describe your app conversationally
2. **Full-Stack Generation** - Frontend, backend, database, auth
3. **Instant Deployment** - Running on a URL immediately
4. **Iterative Refinement** - Describe changes, regenerate
