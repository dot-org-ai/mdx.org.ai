# StoryBrand Framework: yc.startups.org.ai

## 1. Character (The Hero)

**Who is our customer?**

The Founder, Investor, or Job Seeker who wants to explore the YC ecosystem more effectively.

**What do they want?**
- To find relevant YC companies quickly
- Insights beyond basic company info
- Trends and patterns in the YC portfolio
- Better search than the official directory

## 2. Problem

### External Problem (Tangible)
"The YC company database is huge but hard to search. I can't find what I'm looking for."

### Internal Problem (Emotional)
"I know there are amazing companies in here but the discovery experience is frustrating."

### Philosophical Problem (Why It's Wrong)
The world's most valuable startup dataset deserves the world's best discovery experience.

### The Villain
**Poor Discovery** - A treasure trove of startup data locked behind basic search and limited filters.

## 3. Guide (The Brand)

**Position:** We've built the YC company explorer that should have existed all along.

**Empathy:** We've spent hours scrolling through YC companies trying to find the right ones. We built this for ourselves.

**Authority:**
- Complete YC company database
- AI-powered semantic search
- Trend analysis and insights
- Real-time updates from YC

## 4. Plan (The Path)

### Step 1: Search
Use natural language to describe what you're looking for. AI understands context.

### Step 2: Explore
Browse by batch, industry, stage, or any combination. Discover unexpected connections.

### Step 3: Analyze
See trends, comparisons, and insights across the YC portfolio.

## 5. Call to Action

**Direct CTA:** Explore YC Companies

**Transitional CTA:** See Latest Batch

## 6. Success (What's at Stake)

**Success looks like:**
- Finding relevant companies in seconds
- Discovering opportunities you'd have missed
- Understanding YC trends and patterns
- Efficient research and due diligence

**Transformation:**
From: Frustrated YC database user
To: Efficient YC ecosystem explorer

## 7. Failure (What We Help Them Avoid)

**Failure looks like:**
- Hours scrolling through irrelevant results
- Missing companies that were perfect matches
- No visibility into trends and patterns
- Incomplete research due to poor tools

## 8. Brand Identity

### Voice
Efficient, insightful, comprehensive

### Tone
Helpful, analytical, empowering

### Key Messages
1. "Search YC like you think."
2. "The YC explorer that should exist."
3. "Discover the companies you're looking for."
4. "Insights from 4,000+ startups."

## 9. Features

### Search
- Natural language queries
- Semantic matching
- Advanced filters
- Saved searches

### Browse
- By batch (S24, W24, etc.)
- By industry
- By stage
- By location

### Insights
- Batch comparisons
- Industry trends
- Funding patterns
- Growth indicators

## 10. Messaging by Audience

### For Founders
"Find YC companies in your space. Learn from their journeys. Avoid their mistakes."

### For Investors
"Efficient YC portfolio exploration. Find companies that match your thesis."

### For Job Seekers
"Discover the fastest-growing YC companies. Find your next opportunity."

### For Researchers
"Complete YC data with powerful analysis tools. Understand the startup ecosystem."
