# OpenAI

OpenAI is at the forefront of AI research and deployment. While not a YC company directly, it was co-founded by Sam Altman during his tenure as YC President, making it deeply connected to the YC ecosystem.

## The YC Connection

Sam Altman served as President of Y Combinator from 2014 to 2019. In 2015, while still at YC, he co-founded OpenAI with Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, and others. The organization embodies YC's ambitious vision of world-changing technology.

## Key Products

- **GPT-4** - Advanced language model
- **ChatGPT** - Conversational AI interface
- **DALL-E** - Image generation
- **Codex** - Code generation (powers GitHub Copilot)
- **Whisper** - Speech recognition
- **Sora** - Video generation

## Impact on YC

OpenAI's success has influenced the YC ecosystem:
- AI/ML is now the most funded YC category
- Many YC companies build on OpenAI APIs
- YC has increased focus on AI safety startups

## Funding History

- 2015: Founded as non-profit with $1B commitment
- 2019: Created for-profit arm, $1B from Microsoft
- 2023: $10B additional from Microsoft
- 2024: Valued at $80B+
