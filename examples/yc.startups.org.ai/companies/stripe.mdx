# Stripe

Stripe is a technology company that builds economic infrastructure for the internet. Businesses of every size—from new startups to public companies—use their software to accept payments and manage their businesses online.

## The YC Story

Founded by Irish brothers Patrick and John Collison, Stripe was part of Y Combinator's Summer 2009 batch. The company started with a simple idea: make it easy for developers to accept payments online with just a few lines of code.

## Key Products

- **Stripe Payments** - Accept payments from anywhere
- **Stripe Connect** - Payments for platforms and marketplaces
- **Stripe Billing** - Subscription management
- **Stripe Atlas** - Start a company anywhere
- **Stripe Treasury** - Banking-as-a-service
- **Stripe Capital** - Business financing

## Impact

- Powers payments for millions of businesses
- Processes hundreds of billions in payments annually
- Available in 40+ countries
- Supports 135+ currencies

## Lessons Learned

1. **Developer experience matters** - API-first approach won developers
2. **Global from day one** - Built for international expansion
3. **Keep expanding the platform** - From payments to full financial infrastructure
